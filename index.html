LiUna Local 67

Asbestos, Lead & Mold Remediation and Allied Industries

Always Leading The Way

The Asbestos, Lead and Mold Laborers' Local 67 was chartered in 1989 through the Laborers' International Union of North America and is also an affiliate Local Union of the Northern California District Council of Laborers.

Laborers Local 67 is a specialty local representing members throughout the 46 counties in Northern California who perform work in the following areas:

  • Asbestos Abatement

  • Lead Abatement

  • Mold Abatement

  • Associated specialized work requiring personal protection equipment ("PPE") and engineering controls

  • Utility Vault, Lumber Handlers, Scrap Metal Recyclers and Logistics Companies
