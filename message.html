Message from the Business Manager

As your Business Manager, I welcome you to the Laborersâ€™ Local Union 67, Asbestos, Lead and Mold Remediation & Allied Industries website. Born and raised in Mexico, I immigrated to California at a very young age. I joined the Union at 17. By the time I was 20, I became a general foreman and was running projects in the remediation field for asbestos, lead and mold projects. It did not take long to realize that being part of this Union was the right move for me. In 2003, I was hired with Local 67 as a Business Representative and in 2004; I was appointed Business Manager. I have been reelected for four terms, but not a single time have I ever forgotten the labor work I once performed. It is because of all that hard work, I fight harder to make sure you all have better wages and better working conditions and a better contract. A couple of years ago I was given the the opportunity to represent members in the Utility Vault Industries, Lumber Handlers, Scrap Metal Recyclers and Logistics Companies thus becoming Allied Industries. I continue to work hard for each of the members I represent because I have been where they are. I will diligently fight side by side to assure better living conditions we all deserve.


Victor Parra

Business Manager / Secretary Treasurer
